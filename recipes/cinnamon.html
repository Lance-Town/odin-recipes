Homemade Cinnamon Rolls

Description

Cinnamon rolls are a delicious dessert, and are an easy treat to bake. Below is how to make them yourself at home. From rolling the dough to applying the icing, learn all about how to woo your guests.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Quick Rise Yeast
  • Butter
  • Egg + Egg Yolk
  • Bread Flower
  • Brown Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Cream Cheese
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Vanilla

Steps

Credit goes to ambitious kitchen for the recipe!

  1. Warm milk to 110 degrees F.
  2. Knead dough on the stand mixer with a dough hook.
  3. Transfer dough to a well oiled bowl, and let rise for an hour.
  4. Transfer dough to a well-floured surface and roll into a 14x9 ince rectangle. Spread butter over the dough.
  5. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon in a bown, then sprinkle it over the dough.
  6. Tightly roll the dough, starting from 9 inch side.
  7. Cut 1 inch sections.
  8. Place rolls on a greased pan, cover with plastic wrap and a warm towel, and let rise for half an hour.
  9. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Remove the plastic wrap and towel, and cook for 20-25 minutes. ALlow them to cool for 10 minutes before applying icing.
  10. In a mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. Beat until smoothe and fluffy and then applu over the rolls.
